## About The Project

OpenEpops is a platform designed for flight simulators, providing real VFR and IFR data and tools for airports, airfields, and more across Italy. Enhance your flight simulation experience with accurate, real-world data. All data provided is open source and freely accessible.

## Main Features

1. **Real Data:** Access real VFR and IFR navigation data for airports, airfields, waypoints and more across Italy.

2. **Flight Simulation:** Enjoy a realistic flight experience in your simulator with authentic navigation scenarios powered by our data.

3. **Community Collaboration:** Join our community to contribute and improve the platform for all aviation enthusiasts.

## Data Sources

- Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Public Data
- OpenNav
- European Space Agency (ESA) Satellite Maps
- United States Geological Survey (USGS) Satellite Maps

## Users of Our Data

Our data has been utilized by various projects and platforms, including:

- OurAirports
- OpenFlightMaps
