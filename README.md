## About The Project

Welcome to OpenEpops, the new project that brings a breath of freshness to the world of Italian aviation! OpenEpops is an open-source hangar/virtual airport, designed to provide an accessible and free platform for aviation enthusiasts. Here, you will find a vast dataset with VFR and IFR navigation information, freely usable, sourced from open platforms such as Wikipedia, OurAirports, OpenFlightMaps, the public data of the Federal Aviation Administration, OpenNav, and OpenStreetMap. All information has been carefully verified using satellite maps provided by ESA and USGS.

## Main Features

1. **Complete Dataset:** OpenEpops provides a comprehensive dataset with VFR (Visual Flight Rules) and IFR (Instrument Flight Rules) navigation information, allowing you to virtually explore the Italian airspace. All data comes from open sources and is made available for users free of charge.

2. **Realistic Simulation with FlightGear:** The flight simulation is powered by the renowned open-source simulator FlightGear. We offer a realistic and engaging flight experience, allowing you to explore virtual scenarios based on the data present in our dataset.

3. **Open Source Aircraft Models:** We are actively working on creating open-source aircraft models, open to enthusiastic builders. These models will be available digitally on FlightGear, offering a wide range of options for aviation enthusiasts.

4. **Accessibility and Democratization:** OpenEpops aims to reduce economic and access barriers in the Italian aviation sector. We provide a platform that makes public information and flight simulation resources accessible to anyone interested.

5. **Airport Refinement:** We want to improve all Italian airports data within the flight simulators. This includes realistic layouts to replicate the unique characteristics of each airport.

6. **IFR Navigation Data:** We strive to enhance the IFR navigation data for the Italian region, ensuring that "wanna be pilots" have access to accurate and up-to-date information for a more authentic flying simulator experience. This involves refining waypoints, airways, and other essential navigation elements.

7. **IFR Air Navigation Support Platforms:** To aid pilots in their IFR flights, we are developing user-friendly support platforms that provide valuable information, charts, and resources for effective air navigation. These tools will be designed to assist both beginners and experienced aviators.

8. **Italian-Made Freeware:** As part of our commitment to the FlightGear community, we plan to introduce a variety of freeware aircraft and liveries, specifically showcasing the rich aviation heritage of Italy. This includes the development of Italian-themed aircraft and liveries that users can enjoy without any cost.

9. **Community Collaboration:** The OpenEpops Project is an open-source initiative, and we welcome contributions from enthusiasts, developers, and aviation enthusiasts. Whether you have a passion for scenery design, programming, or aviation, there's a place for you to contribute and make a positive impact on the project.

The project officially launches in 2024, and we invite aviation enthusiasts from around the world to join us on this exciting journey. Let's work together to elevate the immersive virtual flying environment for all.
